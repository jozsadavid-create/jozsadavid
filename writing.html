About the author

Józsa Dávid is a writer, photographer, journalist and creative director. Always writing, before, during and after everything else.

The result is writing that doesn't respect limits. It carries the rhythm of journalism, the framing of photography, the precision of creative direction, and the obsession with detail that comes from years spent behind the bars of one's own conscience.

I've started writing before the internet was a thing. Every word is mine. No AI was, were or will be used, consulted, or invited anywhere near my writing: not in the past, not now, not ever. What you read is the direct and unfiltered output of one brain. My brain. Blame only me.
